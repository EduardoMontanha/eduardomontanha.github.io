What is cryptocurrency?
Cryptocurrency is a decentralized digital currency. No state can influence the cryptocurrency exchange rate, and no state can control the transactions.
Due to encrypted data protection mechanisms it is impossible to obtain information from parties involved in transactions.
What is bitcoin?
Bitcoin is a consensus network that enables a new payment system using digital money. It is the first decentralized peer-to-peer payment network that is powered by its users with no central authority. From a user perspective, bitcoin is pretty much like cash for the Internet.
What is a Cryptocurrency Exchange?
A cryptocurrency exchange is a service to exchange digital currencies, such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, and more, to fiat (paper) currencies or to other digital currencies. You can exchange your fiat currency, such as dollars or Euro, for several other cryptocurrencies. You can also exchange your Bitcoin to fiat.
